Ingredients:
 - 1 cup of beef mince
 - 1 tablespoon of chopped parsley
 - 1 tablespoon of ground garlic
 - 1 tablespoon paprika powder
 - 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
 - 1/2 cup vegetable oil

Directions:
 
 1. In a large bowl, combine the beef mince, chopped parsley, groun garlic, paprika powder, salt and 1 teaspoon of vegetable oil
    and mix until smooth.
 2.	form mince into round balls and the slightly flatten. 
 3. put some vegetable oil in a frying pan and make sure the pan is at medium heat.
 4. place patties in pan and brown both sides 4-5 minutes each side.
 4. Preheat oven to 250 degrees C
 3. Bake 10 minutes or until burger is cooked through completely.
